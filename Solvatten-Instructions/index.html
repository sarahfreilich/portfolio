1. Open all the black and white caps.

2. Fill SOLVATTEN® with water through the openings with the black caps. Pre-filter if the water is muddy.

3. Close all caps. Press the yellow button and open SOLVATTEN®. The indicator will turn red.

4. Face the inside of SOLVATTEN® towards the sun for 2-6 hours.

5. When the indicator turns green the water is ready to use.

6. Open the white caps to pour clean water.
